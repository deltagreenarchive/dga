##### RESEARCH ROLL MODIFIER
- +20%
	- Searching government records when the particular city or department is known
- -20%
	- Searching government records when the Agent knows the country but not the state or city
- -20%
	- Searching a foreign source and the Agent fails to make an applicable language skill roll

##### RESEARCH ROLL RESULTS
- Critical success
	- Find 1D4+1 pieces of information in minimal time: a single hour or day
- Success
	- Find 1D4+1 pieces of information
- Failure
	- Find one piece of information and gain 1 [[Corruption]]
- Fumble
	- As with a failure and experience a manifestation: see [[03- Night Floors Manifestations]] on page 104 of Impossible Landscapes
