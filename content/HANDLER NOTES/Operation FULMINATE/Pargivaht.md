National Park Rangers are college-educated and have standard federal law enforcement training and pow ers. The rangers assist Agents whose presence and actions seem official and legitimate. Most rangers, unless overcome by temporary insanity, will fight to the death to protect a lost child. They wear pistols, survival knives, pepper spray (used mostly to deter wild animals), and body armor, and have a few rifles and shotguns in a small, locked armory in the chief ranger’s office at Trailhead Station.
